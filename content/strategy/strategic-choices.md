# 03 — Strategic Choices

## 3.1 Winning Aspiration

**Internal:** Build a team that punches above its weight. Stable, sustainable revenue with recurring streams.

**External:** 30% market share in home region. Brand perception: "a company we trust and depend on." First call for any international player entering MENA.

**Home region phases:** Egypt/North Africa/Gulf/Pakistan → +Africa → +Europe → Global

### Africa — The Greenfield Opportunity

Africa represents the most natural EAAS expansion target after MENA:
- Growing cement demand driven by urbanization
- Significant waste volumes with usable caloric value
- Markets that cannot afford CapEx but can afford service contracts
- Egypt experience is directly transferable
- **Zero EAAS competitors** in Sub-Saharan Africa
- The Fort model provides the localization playbook

Research has identified **6 priority markets**: Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, and Cameroon.

### Markets Deprioritized

| Market | Barrier |
|--------|---------|
| Morocco | French companies dominate; mature market |
| Turkey | Highly advanced; competes with Gebtron in Iraq |
| Iran | Sanctions forced self-sufficiency |

## 3.3 How to Win

### The EAAS Competitive Weapon

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Supply side | Long-term MSW agreements with municipalities (15 years) |
| Processing | Build/operate RDF production facility |
| Demand side | Energy supply agreement with cement factory |
| Value proposition | 25% energy cost savings; zero CapEx for client |
| Revenue model | Monthly recurring revenue tied to energy delivered |

**Why competitors cannot copy:** No year-round local presence to operate. Can't customize. Can't service. No simultaneous relationships with municipalities AND cement factories.

### Four Business Models Operating Simultaneously

| Model | Description | Horizon |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1. RDF Production Equipment | Sell machines to municipalities | H1 |
| 2. Bulk Handling Equipment | Sell to cement/mining | H1 |
| 3. Waste Facility Management | Operate facilities, produce RDF | H2 |
| 4. **Energy-as-a-Service** | Build, operate, sell energy | **H2 (Priority)** |
