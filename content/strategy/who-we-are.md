## 1.1 Our Identity

Gebtron is a **system integrator** specializing in bulk material handling for heavy industries — cement, mining, petrochemicals, fertilizers, and steel. The company does not manufacture machines; it designs complete material handling systems by combining equipment from multiple European partners into turnkey solutions.

### The Two-Entity Model

| Entity | Location | Role |
|--------|----------|------|
| **Gebtron FZCO** | Dubai, UAE | Headquarters. Sales, strategy, Gulf-facing operations. |
| **Alex Fort Worth (AFW)** | Alexandria, Egypt | Engineering design hub, construction, aftersales. The operational engine. |

Together, they occupy **two of the five business model archetypes** in the global machinery industry: Equipment & System Provider (Gebtron) and Aftersales & Turnkey Solutions Provider (AFW).

## 1.2 Our Values

**Trust & Dependability > Innovation.** Innovation continues, but on Gebtron's own R&D budget — not on client projects.

**Fulfillment & Customization.** Gebtron fulfills requirements completely with flexibility where competitors are rigid.

**Win-Win Over Zero-Sum.** "The market is not a Roman arena. We can create a win-win situation."

**قدّم السبت تلاقي الحد — Lead with Value.** Strategic generosity before expectation. Trust earned through demonstrated good faith.

**Culture of Care.** Medical insurance for employee + spouse + parents. Honeymoon gifts. Remote-flexible culture. Extended family model.

**Pragmatic Sustainability.** Profit first, but products happen to serve environmental goals — locality and availability over ideology.
