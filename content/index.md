# Gebtron Strategic Plan 2026–2030

**From Equipment Supplier to Regional Energy-as-a-Service Leader**

---

## The Briefing

Gebtron stands at a strategic inflection point. The global industrial machinery sector is transforming — from product-centric to solutions-centric, from transactional sales to recurring services. These shifts create exceptional opportunity for a regionally-positioned system integrator with deep local presence and emerging expertise in alternative fuels.

### Our Strategic Thesis

> **Gebtron will transition from an equipment supplier to the leading Energy-as-a-Service (EAAS) provider in the MENA region, leveraging its unique position at the intersection of waste management and industrial energy consumption.**

### Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Partner Heritage | 162+ years (Pfeiffer, Kockele, Kukla) |
| Project Scale | EUR 12-15M cement mill tenders |
| Market Share Target | 30% in home region by 2030 |
| EAAS Profit Multiple | 43× vs. one-time equipment sale |

### Three Horizons

| Horizon | Timeframe | Focus |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| **H1: Defend & Extend** | 0-2 years | Core bulk handling, aftermarket, Egypt + UAE |
| **H2: Build & Scale** | 2-5 years | EAAS launch, European infrastructure, Saudi, AI analytics |
| **H3: Explore & Position** | 5+ years | Africa via EAAS, digital platform, Fort model expansion |
