# European Expansion — Integrated Recommendation

> Acquire a German target. Base operations in Poland. Hold through Netherlands BV. Budget EUR 500K-1M equity and 12-24 months.

*⏱ 5 min read*
**Audience:** CEO, Board, Strategy Bootcamp
**Date:** April 15, 2026

---

## The Integrated Strategy

Three moves, executed in sequence:

### Move 1: Find and Acquire a German Target (12-24 months)

**What to buy:**
- Small German machinery/equipment manufacturer
- Revenue EUR 3-8M, EBITDA EUR 0.5-2M, 15-60 employees
- Owner aged 60+ with no identified successor
- IP, customer relationships, established brand
- Relevant to Gebtron's sectors: bulk handling, conveying, weighing, cement technology, alternative fuels equipment

**How to finance (the SBA equivalent):**
```
EUR 5M deal:
  10% (EUR 500K) — Gebtron equity
  10% (EUR 500K) — Seller note / earn-out
  10% (EUR 500K) — MBG silent participation
  70% (EUR 3.5M) — KfW-backed bank loan + Bürgschaftsbank guarantee
```

**Where to look:**
1. Register on nexxt-change.org and DUB.de now
2. Engage KERN (succession specialist, 30+ DACH offices) for buyer-side search
3. Contact IHK Stuttgart, NRW, Munich succession advisors
4. Budget 12-24 months; review 20-50 targets

**What Gebtron adds to the target:**
- MENA + Africa market access (the acquired company can sell to Gebtron's client base)
- Operational revenue from day 1 (Gebtron's existing projects flow through the European entity)
- Growth that the retiring owner couldn't provide
- The acquired business pays off the loan from its own cash flows + Gebtron-generated revenue

### Move 2: Establish Poland Operations Base (6-12 months)

**What to build:**
- Warehouse/staging hub for European-sourced components
- CE marking and European certification capability
- Light assembly and packaging
- Shipping consolidation point to MENA and Africa

**Where:** Wroclaw or Katowice (near German border, strong logistics, PIZ incentives)

**Cost:**
- Setup: EUR 15-30K (company formation + initial fit-out)
- Annual operations: EUR 150-250K (rent, 3-4 staff, utilities)
- CIT: 9% for small taxpayers (revenue <EUR 2M)

**Why Poland over Germany:**
- 42% of Germany's operating costs
- 1-3 day online company formation
- PIZ offers 40-70% CIT exemption
- UAE-Poland DTA in force (5% dividend WHT)
- GECOS already uses a Polish workshop — knowledge and contacts exist

### Move 3: Structure Through Netherlands BV

**Why:**
- UAE-Germany DTA expired (2021) → 26.375% WHT on dividends from German GmbH to UAE
- NL BV participation exemption = 0% tax on dividends from German subsidiary
- EU Parent-Subsidiary Directive = 0% WHT from German GmbH to NL BV
- NL-UAE DTA = 0-5% WHT from NL BV to Gebtron FZCO

**Setup:** EUR 3-5K; annual maintenance EUR 5-15K. This saves EUR 130K+/year on a EUR 500K profit stream.
