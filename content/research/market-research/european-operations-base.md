## The Germany Tax Problem

**Critical finding: The UAE-Germany Double Taxation Agreement (DTA) expired on December 31, 2021 and has NOT been renewed.**

| Scenario | With DTA (pre-2022) | Without DTA (current) |
|----------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Dividend WHT | 5% (≥10% holding) | **~26.375%** |
| Interest WHT | 0% | Up to 15% |
| Capital gains | Exempt | May be taxable |

**Impact:** Dividends from a German GmbH to Gebtron FZCO (UAE) now face ~26.375% withholding tax. For a company generating EUR 500K profit, that's EUR 132K lost to German WHT — annually.

**Solution:** Route through a **Netherlands BV** (0% participation exemption on received dividends, 0% WHT on dividends to UAE under NL-UAE treaty) or **Cyprus Ltd** (budget option).

```
Gebtron FZCO (UAE — 0% CIT)
    └── Netherlands BV (0% participation exemption)
            ├── German GmbH (if acquired)
            ├── Polish Sp. z o.o. (operations base)
            └── Other EU entities
```

### Tax Treaties with UAE

| Country | DTA Status | Dividend WHT (≥10% holding) | Interest WHT |
|---------|-----------|---------------------------|-------------|
| **Germany** | **EXPIRED (Dec 2021)** | **~26.375%** | Up to 15% |
| Austria | In force (2003) | **0%** | 0% |
| Poland | In force (2013) | **5%** | 0-5% |
| Czech Republic | In force (2023) | **5%** | 0% |
| Portugal | In force (2011) | **5%** | 0-10% |
| **Netherlands** | In force | **0-5%** | 0% |

**Austria is the only country with 0% dividend WHT to UAE** — but at higher operating costs. Poland and Czech Republic at 5% are excellent. Germany without a DTA is punitive.

## Recommended Operations Base

### Primary: Poland (Wroclaw or Katowice)

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Cost** | 42% of Germany; lowest salaries and rent in comparison |
| **Setup** | 1-3 days online; PLN 5,000 minimum capital |
| **Tax** | 19% CIT (9% for small); PIZ offers 40-70% CIT exemption |
| **UAE DTA** | In force; 5% dividend WHT |
| **Logistics** | 6-8h drive to German suppliers; Gdansk port for MENA shipping |
| **Talent** | Strong engineering workforce; 22-35K EUR for mechanical engineer |
| **GECOS connection** | GECOS already uses a Polish workshop — knowledge exists |
| **Language** | English improving; German speakers available near border |

### Why Not Germany as Primary Base

Despite having the best brand value and deepest supplier access:
1. **No UAE DTA** — 26.375% dividend WHT is destructive
2. **30% CIT** — highest in the comparison
3. **Highest operating costs** — 2-3x Poland/Czech
4. **4-8 week company formation** — bureaucratic

**Germany should be the acquisition target (for IP and heritage), not the operations base.** "Engineered in Germany, Assembled in the EU" preserves brand value while capturing CEE cost advantages.

### Alternative: Czech Republic

If Poland doesn't work out, Czech Republic is an excellent fallback:
- CZK 1 minimum capital, 19 days formation
- CzechInvest offers 10-year CIT holiday
- Prague is 4 hours from Munich
- Strong precision engineering heritage (Brno/Moravia)
- 5% dividend WHT to UAE under 2023 treaty
