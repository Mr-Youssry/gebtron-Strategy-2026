# GECOS GmbH — Company Profile

## Company Overview

**GECOS GmbH** is a German engineering company specializing in the design and sale of machinery and industrial equipment for bulk material conveying technology. The company develops customized solutions for various bulk materials in industrial applications.

## Core Business & Capabilities

GECOS GmbH specializes in:

### Bulk Material Conveying Technology
- Development of customized solutions for various bulk materials in industrial applications
- Keeping pace with changes and innovations in bulk materials

### In-House Laboratory
The company has its own bulk materials laboratory equipped with:
- **Translational shear device**
- **Ring shear cell**
- **Time consolidation bench**

This enables the determination of relevant values for bulk materials, which can then be incorporated into the optimal design of conveying technology components.

## Products (Licensed from Louise Engineering Limited)

GECOS GmbH holds manufacturing and distribution licenses for:
- **SDM** - Silo Discharge Machine
- **BMD** - Bunker Discharge Machine
- **RDM** - Rotary Discharge Machine
- **TRU** - Truck Reception Unit
- **Sweeping Auger**
- **Chain Conveyor**
- **Design Programs** for machines

### License Terms
- 2% license fee on net invoice amounts for machine sales
- 5% license fee for spare parts
- Agreement can be terminated with 3 months' notice to end of quarter
