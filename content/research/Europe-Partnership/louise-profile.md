# Louise Engineering Limited — Company Profile

## Company Overview

**Louise Engineering Limited** is a UK-based engineering company specializing in the maintenance, servicing, and repair of technical equipment and machinery for industrial, commercial, and other applications.

## Business Purpose

The company's purpose is the maintenance, servicing, and repair of technical equipment, machinery, and other equipment that is not clearly assigned to a specific repair industry. This includes:

- Repair, maintenance, and functional assurance of equipment of various types
- Equipment used in industrial, commercial, or other areas

## Products & Intellectual Property

Louise Engineering Limited is the proprietor of the following products, for which it has granted GECOS GmbH a manufacturing and distribution license (dated 28 August 2018):

- **SDM** - Silo Discharge Machine
- **BMD** - Bunker Discharge Machine
- **RDM** - Rotary Discharge Machine
- **TRU** - Truck Reception Unit
- **Sweeping Auger**
- **Chain Conveyor**
- **Programs** for the design of the machines

## Business Model

The company operates primarily through:

1. **License Agreement with GECOS GmbH**: Louise Engineering Limited provides confidential technical knowledge, know-how, design documentation, and drawings. It also grants online access to the drawing index. The licensee (GECOS GmbH) pays:
   - 2% license fee on net invoice amounts for machine sales
   - 5% license fee for spare parts

2. **Sale-and-Lease-Back Agreement**: In February 2022, the company sold a calculation program for machine design to GECOS GmbH for EUR 35,000 and leased it back at EUR 3,500 per year (10-year term).

The company does not engage in its own operational business activities and has no proprietary production or distribution structures.
