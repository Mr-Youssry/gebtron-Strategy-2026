## Strategic Partners

### Gebr. Pfeiffer SE — Germany
*⏱ 8 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Preparing for mill partnership discussions, the cement mill tender, or Africa EAAS expansion.

💡 **Key takeaway:** 162-year German company, 15 global subsidiaries (including Egypt & Dubai), recently sold a mill into West Africa (Côte d'Ivoire). Natural integration partner — Pfeiffer supplies the mill, Gebtron integrates the full system. EAAS pitch: make Pfeiffer mills accessible to factories that can't afford upfront CapEx.

## Merger Targets (MPPG Holding / Assignment 01)

### GECOS GmbH — Germany
*⏱ 5 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Reviewing the GECOS/Louise merger terms or discussing the MPPG deal structure.

💡 **Key takeaway:** German bulk material conveying company, EUR 4M revenue, growing EBITDA (62.9% CAGR). Holds manufacturing licenses from Louise Engineering. Valued at EUR 1.84M (Oct 2025). Owned by Peter Gasch and Mykola Popovych via MPPG Holding.

- [[gecos-profile|Full Company Profile →]]

### Louise Engineering Limited — United Kingdom
*⏱ 4 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Reviewing the IP licensing structure or negotiating IP terms in the MPPG deal.

💡 **Key takeaway:** UK-based IP holder (SDM, BMD, RDM machines). EUR 93K revenue from 2% / 5% license fees. Valued at EUR 870K (Oct 2025). Pure IP licensing entity — no operations. High equity ratio (91%). Owned by same MPPG structure as GECOS.

## Acquisition Candidates (Assignment 02 — European Expansion)

### GöMa Göritzhainer Maschinenfabrik — Germany
*⏱ 6 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Preparing for European acquisition strategy, or before any trade show contact with GöMa.

💡 **Key takeaway:** Sole proprietorship in Saxony. Specialises in **continuous weighing and dosing** — the exact missing link in a Pfeiffer mill integration. 2nd generation (Jürgen Machan) took over in 2022. Estimated deal value EUR 1.5–2.5M, requiring only EUR 150–250K equity from Gebtron. **Highest-priority acquisition target in the pipeline.** Approach: distribution partnership first, minority stake second.
