## Key takeaway
Gartner's 2025 editions put **generative AI in the Trough of Disillusionment**, while **digital twins, IoT platforms, and MOM suites are climbing out of the trough** toward productive use. **AI agents and AI-ready data** are the fastest-rising items at the peak. **Autonomous mobile robots and cobots** are already on the slope (2–5 years to mainstream); **humanoid robots** are pure hype (10+ years). For Gebtron, bet on desk-layer AI and design-layer digital twins now; research, don't deploy, site-layer humanoids.

## Executive Summary
Gartner released 20+ Hype Cycles in 2025. Six matter for Gebtron: Manufacturing Operations Strategy, Discrete Manufacturing, AI, GenAI, Intralogistics Smart Robots & Drones, and Emerging Technologies. Three signals are consistent:

1. **GenAI has peaked.** <30% of CEOs are satisfied with ROI; it has entered the Trough. Value capture now requires AI Engineering, ModelOps, and AI-ready data — not model novelty.
2. **Foundational Industry 4.0 tech is productive.** IoT platforms, digital twins, MOM/MES suites are exiting the Trough — 2026–2028 is the deployment window.
3. **Embodied AI and humanoids are the new hype peak.** Cobots and AMRs are shipping; humanoids and embodied AI are 10+ years from scale.

## What the Hype Cycle is
Gartner plots public expectation vs. time in five phases: **Innovation Trigger** (first demos, no products), **Peak of Inflated Expectations** (unrealistic promises, early failures), **Trough of Disillusionment** (press turns negative, weak vendors die), **Slope of Enlightenment** (second-generation products deliver value, best practices emerge), **Plateau of Productivity** (mainstream adoption, boring and profitable). Lesson: buying at the Peak is expensive and failure-prone; buying on the Slope is where disciplined firms make money.

## Emerging Tech Hype Cycle — industrial relevance
From the 2025 Emerging Technologies Hype Cycle, three themes matter to Gebtron:

- **Augmented Humanity** (wearable AI, AR/MR, human-robot collaboration). Highest near-term ROI (6–18 months) per Gartner SMEs — "the most concrete area." Supports the design and site layers of the AI-Augmented Engineer thesis.
- **Autonomous Business** (AI agents, machine customers, self-adaptive products). Medium-term (12–24 months) pilot candidates; heavy governance burden.
- **Hypermachinery** (fully agentic factories). "Immature, expensive, very high risk" — research only, do not budget for 2026–2027.

Also tracked: AGI and embodied AI (peak), brain-machine interfaces (slope, narrow use), quantum (10+ years), AI safety/regulation (2–5 years, EU AI Act-driven).

## Production-ready vs. peak-of-hype

**Buy now (slope/plateau):** digital twins for specific assets (pumps, kilns, conveyors); IoT/MOM/MES platforms; cobots in bounded cells; AMRs for intralogistics; computer vision for quality inspection; AR work instructions on smart glasses; GenAI copilots for desk work.

**Pilot cautiously (peak):** AI agents in production workflows; agentic/autonomous manufacturing; industrial metaverse as full-plant experience; drone-based autonomous inspection.

**Research only (trigger / do not deploy):** humanoid robots on shop floor; embodied AI for general-purpose manipulation; AGI-labelled tools; quantum for operations.

## Where our three layers sit
| Layer | Technologies | Curve position | Gebtron action 2026–2027 |
|-------|--------------|----------------|---------------------------|
| **Desk / second-brain** | GenAI copilots (Claude, Copilot, ChatGPT), AI-ready data, ModelOps | Trough (GenAI) climbing to Slope; AI engineering on Slope | Deploy now. This is where ROI is proven and cheap. |
| **Design / CAD–digital-twin copilots** | Digital twins, IoT platforms, AI-enabled CAD assistants, computer vision | Climbing out of Trough onto Slope | Fund pilots in 2026; standardise in 2027. Foundational tech is maturing — good window. |
| **Site / AR–drones–cobots** | AR smart glasses, wearable AI, cobots, AMRs (if intralogistics relevant), inspection drones | Slope (cobots, AMRs, AR); Peak/Trigger (humanoids, agentic factories) | Targeted pilots in 2026; avoid humanoids and "fully autonomous factory" pitches. |
