# Strategic Frameworks

> The thinking tools behind our strategy. Read these before attending strategy sessions.

---

## Playing to Win
*⏱ 5 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Before any strategy session, or when someone references the "choice cascade."

💡 **Key takeaway:** Strategy is about making choices — 5 cascading questions: Winning Aspiration → Where to Play → How to Win → Capabilities → Management Systems. Developed by A.G. Lafley (P&G) and Roger Martin.

## Three Horizons
*⏱ 5 min read*

📌 **Read when:** Discussing resource allocation across current business vs. future bets.

💡 **Key takeaway:** H1 (defend core), H2 (build emerging), H3 (explore future). Our allocation: 55/40/5 financial, 40/50/10 management attention — deliberately overweighting H2.
