# Gebtron - Al Dhafra to Fujairah C&D Waste Deal

## Economic Analysis Summary

**Date:** January 2026
**Prepared by:** Gebtron FZCO
**Status:** Decision Analysis (Pre-Feasibility)

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates the economics of processing Construction & Demolition (C&D) waste from Al Dhafra region, producing RDF (Refuse Derived Fuel) for sale to Fujairah cement factory, with an option to also process dead waste for incineration.

### Two Options Evaluated

| Metric | Option (a): RDF Only | Option (b): Landfill Replacement |
|--------|---------------------|----------------------------------|
| **Scope** | RDF production only | RDF + Dead waste processing |
| **C&D Waste Processed** | 1,000 t/day | 2,500 t/day |
| **Total CAPEX** | AED 40.9M | AED 48.2M |
| **Annual Revenue** | AED 37.5M | AED 46.5M |
| **Annual OPEX** | AED 22.2M | AED 30.3M |
| **EBITDA** | AED 15.3M | AED 16.2M |
| **Net Income** | AED 10.7M | AED 10.9M |
| **Equity Payback** | **1.57 years** | **1.91 years** |

### Recommendation

**Option (a)** delivers faster equity payback (1.57 vs 1.91 years) with lower capital risk. However, **Option (b)** provides strategic value through:
- Stronger relationship with Al Dhafra (full waste solution)
- Higher absolute returns (AED 10.9M vs 10.7M net income)
- Landfill diversion alignment with UAE sustainability goals

## Deal Structure

### Waste Flow

```
Al Dhafra C&D Waste (5,000 t/day)
          │
          ├── 50% Recyclables (2,500 t/day) → Sold by Al Dhafra
          │
          ├── 20% RDF Feedstock (1,000 t/day) → Gebtron RDF Plant → Fujairah Cement
          │
          └── 30% Dead Waste (1,500 t/day) → Option (b) only → Fujairah Incinerator
```

### Pricing Structure (All Ex-Works)

| Item | Rate | Paid By | Paid To |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| RDF Processing Fee | AED 5/t | Al Dhafra | Gebtron |
| Dead Waste Processing Fee | AED 20/t | Al Dhafra | Gebtron |
| RDF Sale Price | AED 120/t | Fujairah Cement | Gebtron |
| Incineration Fee | AED 3/t | Gebtron | Fujairah Cement |

**Note:** All prices are Ex-Works. Transportation is buyer's responsibility.

## Key Formulas Reference

| Calculation | Formula |
|-------------|---------|
| Annual Depreciation | (CAPEX × 90%) ÷ 15 years |
| Average Principal Payment | Loan Amount ÷ 10 years |
| Average Interest Payment | Loan × Rate × 0.55 |
| EBT | EBITDA - Depreciation - Interest |
| Net Income | EBT × (1 - Tax Rate) |
| Distribution | Net Income - Principal Payment |
| Payback Period | Equity ÷ Distribution |
