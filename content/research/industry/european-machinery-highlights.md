### For PESTEL Analysis

#### Political
- European integration supports cross-border operations (p.70)
- Need for bureaucratic efficiency (39% cite as issue) (p.67)

#### Economic
- Traditional market growth slowing—shift from 30% to 21% as growth driver (p.32)
- BRIC slowdown affecting export-dependent companies (p.32)
- Economic volatility rated top organizational driver (45%) (p.48)

#### Social
- War for talent—software development (54%), mechanical engineering (53%), mechatronics (52%) critical skills (p.54)
- Only 34% have international management despite 66% export sales (p.53)
- Workforce largely domestic (77%) despite international sales (p.27)

#### Technological
- Industry 4.0 / digitization transforming business models (p.38)
- Four digital model types emerging: platform, as-a-service, IPR-based, data-driven (p.39)
- Digital implementation gaps: customization (49%) vs. supply/demand matching (10%) (p.44)

#### Environmental
- Sustainability not prominently featured in this study (minimal coverage)

#### Legal
- Data security concerns barrier to digitization (29%, rising to 44% for software providers) (p.46)
- IP protection important for IPR-based business models (p.39)

## 4. Recommendations for Gebtron/GECOS Analysis

### Positioning Assessment
Based on these benchmarks, evaluate GECOS against:

1. **Archetype Classification**: Where does GECOS fit?
   - If primarily components → expect 10.2% EBIT, 5.6% CAGR
   - If primarily equipment/systems → expect 8.2% EBIT, 8.3% CAGR
   - If moving toward aftersales → expect 10.7% EBIT, 4.8% CAGR

2. **Scale Considerations**:
   - GECOS at EUR 4M is well below survey average (EUR 476M)
   - Pre-scale margin expectations: ~10% EBIT
   - Merger could accelerate path to scale benefits

3. **Vertical Integration Strategy**:
   - If GECOS resembles component specialist → high integration adds value
   - If GECOS resembles equipment provider → avoid excessive integration

4. **Digital Readiness**:
   - Industry lagging (<20% adoption)
   - 5-10% cost reduction opportunity via operational digitization
   - >10% revenue potential from digital models by 2020
