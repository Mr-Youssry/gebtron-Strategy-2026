## Valuation Methods

### German Valuation Method (Ertragswertverfahren)
*⏱ 8 min read*

📌 **Read when:** You're evaluating a German company or reviewing a valuation report from RSM Ebner Stolz.

💡 **Key takeaway:** The Income Value Method per IDW S1 standard. Two-phase model with step-by-step process, formulas, and a replication checklist.

- [[german-valuation-method|Full Methodology Guide →]]

### Understanding Beta, WACC & Discount Rates
*⏱ 7 min read*

📌 **Read when:** You're negotiating valuation terms or reviewing discount rate assumptions.

💡 **Key takeaway:** WACC blends cost of equity and debt. Small changes in discount rate dramatically affect valuation. Includes negotiation points.

- [[beta-wacc-discount-rates|Practical Guide with Examples →]]

### Cash Flows in DCF Valuation
*⏱ 7 min read*

📌 **Read when:** You're building a DCF model or choosing between FCFF, FCFE, and DDM approaches.

💡 **Key takeaway:** Decision framework for selecting the right cash flow type. Covers working capital, CapEx, terminal value, and the German Earnings Value Method.
